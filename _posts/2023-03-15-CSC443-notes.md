# CSC443 Notes
## Raid
### Raid 0
Pure striping. Splitting logical addressing to multiple disk.
### Raid 1
Mirroring, each drive has a mirror(copy).
### Raid 0+1
Striping and mirroring.
## Table Management
### Supported Operations
    1. Scan
    2. Deletes
    3. Updates
    4. Insertions
